How to Deposit into CaltechAUTHORS

This form lets you deposit an article into the CaltechAUTHORS repository. It is intended to be a shortcut for basic submissions. The full deposit page is located here (requires login).

FieldDescriptionRequired?
Title The title of the item. The title should not end with a period, but may end with a question mark. There is no way to make italic text, please enter it normally. If you have a subtitle, it should be preceded with a colon [:]. Use capitals only for the first word and for proper nouns.
Example: A brief history of time
Example: Life: an unauthorized biography
Example: Mathematics for engineers and scientists. 5th edition
Example: Ecosystems of the world. Vol. 26. Estuaries of the world

When you click "Deposit Article", the information that you entered, along with the file(s) you attached, will be sent for review by CODA staff. The article will not appear in CaltechAUTHORS until it has been approved. This process typically takes a few business days.
